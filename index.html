Common TV Problems We Fix

TV has sound but no picture

Usually a backlight or inverter issue. We can replace the LEDs safely.

Screen lines or flickering

Often caused by T-Con board faults or panel ribbon cables, which we re-bond.

HDMI port not working

We replace the damaged HDMI ports directly on the motherboard.

TV not turning on or Boot issues

A sign of power supply failure or software corruption we can reprogram.

Display distortion / Colors bleeding

We diagnose whether it is mainboard logic or the LVDS cable and fix it.
